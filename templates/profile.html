How Context Scoring Works

HybridSec uses your business profile to amplify vulnerability scores:

  • E-commerce / Healthcare / Finance: 1.8× risk multiplier
  • Database / App Server: +0.3–0.4 score addition
  • Sensitive customer data: +0.4 addition
  • No IT staff: increases urgency of automated fixes

These weights are fed into the Triple Hybrid Scoring engine (Rule-Based 30% + ML 35% + LLM 35%) to produce a final 0–10 risk score per vulnerability.
